Chrysocolla

I have always been drawn to blue and green gems

so I have been obsessed with this stone since it's been on my work bench. The blue/green color variations naturally form when copper is present during the formation of chrysocolla. The matrix of the host stone provides a contrast that leaves thin veins of color growing throughout the stone that really highlight all the intricate details. This is a beautiful and unique piece of the earth.
